About Me


Hi, I'm Preston Devonshire, a front-end web developer based in Atlanta, GA. I was born and raised in Jonesboro, GA where I lived until I turned 18 and left for college. I attended the University of Alabama where I studied General Business and Computing Technology & Applications.

Shortly after graduation in May of 2016, I moved away from business and toward computers. After taking a couple of classes online, I discovered an interest in web development. Wanting to learn more, I began to teach myself how to code through sites like Codeacademy and Treehouse.

When I'm not working on code, you can find me watching sports or playing video games. I'm an avid college football fan (Roll Tide!) and enjoy spending time with my dog Coco or doing outdoor activities such as playing soccer and swimming.
